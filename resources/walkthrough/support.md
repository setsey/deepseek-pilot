# Support the project

DeepSeek Pilot is free and open source under MIT. If it saves you time or money, a few ways to give back:

- ★ Star the repo on [GitHub](https://github.com/setsey/deepseek-pilot)
- 💖 [Sponsor on GitHub](https://github.com/sponsors/setsey) — even small recurring tips help cover testing costs
- 🐞 [File issues or feature requests](https://github.com/setsey/deepseek-pilot/issues)
- 🗣 Share the extension with anyone running long agentic sessions on DeepSeek

Bug reports with reproduction steps and the **DeepSeek Pilot: Show Logs** output attached are especially welcome.
